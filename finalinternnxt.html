Prodesy Reporting

Our purpose-built software propels businesses into a new age of chemical data analytics and KPI reporting. Prodesy is centered around integration of chemical usage, production and data analysis, customizable analytics, remote monitoring and automation, accurate sales and manufacturing forecasting and analytics and predictive modeling..

Data Management

Prodesy provides a simple platform to capture data once at the point of origin, and avoid double entry and wasted effort. The mobile app helps capture data in the field – inventory, production, pressure, field notes, sample data – this is where the process starts. Lab analysis data is captured in the web platform, where comprehensive, integrated reports are generated without any additional effort. With Prodesy you capture data once – and the program gives you all the management tools and reports you need. .

Learn More

Optimization Platform

Prodesy leverages enterprise data and IoT devices to provide instantaneous optimization capabilities based on multiple process inputs. Treatment optimization is not simply hitting a Target PPM rate – it is adjusting programs based on multiple input factors such as production, pressure, temperature, event data (dewax, etc.) and more, while also incorporating lab analysis data for a complete picture of program performance. Prodesy provides the foundation to continuously optimize treatment programs..

OUR PROJECT EXPERIENCE

Project 1

Deployed inventory management and reporting platform for a specialty chemical vendor to reduce time spent sorting through spreadsheets. The vendor was losing customers due to poor reporting which was not communicating the value of their service and treatment programs, and costing field staff many hours of effort each month. The Embark solution resulted in the equivalent of 30 days/month of time save for field and admin staff, and improved customer satisfaction and retention. Delivery Tickets are now streamlined to invoicing to improve cash flow. The company also realized a 13% reduction in field service costs as a percentage of gross margin..

View Case Study

Project 2

Delivered analytics platform for a substantial chemical program integrating chemical usage data from 7 chemical vendors and production data from production accounting and SCADA. The platform was deployed through the company’s corporate PowerBI environment for seamless access for users at all levels. Program resulted in higher treatment accuracy - variance dropped to 8% versus the previous levels of 15% to 20%. This resulted in longer production runs, lower dewax costs and better asset integrity..

View Case Study

Project 3

Deployed Paraffin Management - Optimization Solution to provide analytics of program efficiency and to optimize programs based on production, pressure, temperature, dewax events and artificial lift. An optimization platform was setup to continuously optimize treatment program based on all factors, with real time data and instantaneous treatment rate adjustments based on program rules developed and fine tuned with past performance. Overall Treatment and Dewax Cost/BOE fell by $0.78, and treatment variance fell to under 5% from and average of 18% prior to solution deployment. Production increased through longer production runs and fewer downtime events, and the solution saved dozens of hours per month of time spent manually adjusting programs to optimize..
